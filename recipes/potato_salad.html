Potato Salad

A bowl of potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 5 Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Stalk of Celery
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes
  2. Fill a pot with water and put in potatoes
  3. Bring the pot with potatoes to a boil
  4. Once the potatoes are soft (can poke a fork through), put in the eggs and boil for 12 minutes
  5. Turn off the heat and drain water
  6. Peel the potatoes and eggs
  7. Mash up the potatoes and dice eggs
  8. Add Mayonnaise, Salt, Pepper, and Paprika to your taste
